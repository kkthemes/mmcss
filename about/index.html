About

Learn about MM Notes, get to know people behind this app and find out guidelines for using MM Notes logo and name.

MM Notes is a minimal modern note taking app which allows you to write in markdown or plain text and automatically saves your edits in browser's cache. You can draft a note, write posts, articles or use it to jot together a todo list.

Primary goal of MM Notes is to be a lightweight utility for note taking in the browser. With the distraction free UI and auto-saving, you can focus easily on your writing.
